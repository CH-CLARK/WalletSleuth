About

Wallet Sleuth is a cryptocurrency discovery and triage tool, designed for the quick and targeted identification of user cryptocurrency artefacts from a variety of popular cryptocurrency wallets!

Identify 🔍
Scan user profiles and parse user cryptocurrency addresses, extended public keys and transaction IDs from selected wallets. Identified artefacts are immediatley displayed in an output window and a process log details actions and errors, both are produced to the output directory for additional work or review.
